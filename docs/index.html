How It Works

1

Hold

Middle-click your mouse, press Ctrl+D, or step on your Stream Deck pedal.

2

Speak

Talk naturally. With streaming enabled, text appears live as you speak. Parakeet TDT transcribes locally on Apple Silicon — nothing leaves your Mac.

3

Paste

Release the trigger. Your text is instantly pasted into whatever app you're using.

Features

🧠

Parakeet TDT Engine

NVIDIA Parakeet TDT v3 — 2.1% word error rate, 3-6x faster than Whisper. Runs on Apple Neural Engine via Core ML. Whisper available as fallback.

⚡
Beta

Streaming Transcription

Real-time partial results while you speak. Text appears live in the overlay as you dictate, powered by FluidAudio's EOU 120M streaming model. Enable in Settings.

🤫

Whisper-quiet speech

No more missed whispers. The pre-model silence gate has been removed — quiet and whispered speech now reaches the transcription engine.

🎨

Cyberpunk Themes

5 built-in overlay themes — TextEcho, Cyber, Classic, Ocean, Sunset — plus full color customization.

🦶

Stream Deck Pedal

Hands-free dictation with Elgato Stream Deck Pedal. Push-to-talk, paste, and enter — all by foot.

📜

Transcription History

Browse, search, copy, and re-use past transcriptions. Stored locally in plain text.

🔒

Privacy First

Zero telemetry. Zero network calls. Audio is processed in memory and never written to disk.

💬

On-Device LLM

6 curated MLX models with 3 modes: Grammar Fix, Rephrase, Answer. Shift+Middle-click to transcribe+process. Pre-send review lets you check text and cycle modes (Ctrl+Shift+M) before sending. ESC cancels generation mid-stream. Runs on GPU — separate from transcription on Neural Engine.

🧙

Setup Wizard

Guided first-launch wizard handles permissions, model download, and pedal setup in under two minutes.
